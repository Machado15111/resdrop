# VOYU — Ad Creative Frameworks
## Meta Ads System | Hotels & Villas in Brazil | US & European Markets

---

## AD STRATEGY OVERVIEW

### The Funnel Architecture:

```
COLD AUDIENCE (Awareness)
  ↓ [Desire-led video + image ads]
WARM AUDIENCE (Consideration)
  ↓ [Trust + curation carousels + testimonials]
HOT AUDIENCE (Conversion/Inquiry)
  ↓ [Direct response: "Plan your stay" + specific property]
PAST VISITORS / INQUIRERS (Remarketing)
  ↓ [Personalized property + destination follow-up]
```

### Key Principle for All Ads:
> Never run a Voyu ad that looks like a travel agency ad. Run ads that look like editorial content from a brand that knows Brazil better than anyone.

## FRAMEWORK 1 — HOTEL-FIRST DEMAND GENERATION

**Goal:** Make someone who hadn't seriously considered a specific hotel or Brazil decide they need to go.

**Target audience:**
- Cold: 30–60 year olds in US/EU, interests: luxury travel, boutique hotels, Condé Nast Traveler, Airbnb Luxe, Mr & Mrs Smith, travel magazines, international destinations
- Lookalike: 1–3% lookalike of website visitors or past inquiries

**Main emotion:** Desire + discovery

**Problem solved:** "I didn't know where to stay in Brazil." / "I've been thinking about Brazil but didn't know if the hotels were actually good."

**Visual concept:** Single property hero shot — architecturally beautiful, warm light, minimal text overlay.

**Headline directions (test 3–5 variants):**
```
A: "Waking up in [specific hotel] in Rio. This is how it starts."
B: "The hotel most people visiting Rio never discover."
C: "There's a version of Brazil that doesn't look like the brochures."
D: "Where to stay in Rio de Janeiro — curated by Voyu."
E: "[Hotel name]. The only address that matters in Santa Teresa."
```

**Primary text directions:**
```
Version A (evocative):
Some hotels are places you stay. Some are places you experience. [Hotel name] in [destination] is the second kind. Tucked into [location detail], it has [specific detail 1] and [specific detail 2] that most travelers only discover by accident. Voyu knows it. And we'll match you to the right stay in Brazil.

Version B (trust + curation):
We've assessed [X] properties across Brazil. [Hotel name] earned its place in our collection because [specific reason 1] and [specific reason 2]. If [destination] is on your list, this is where you should be. See our curated stays → voyutravel.com

Version C (comparison):
If you're choosing between [competitor destination] and Brazil this year, you should know: the hotels and villas in [Brazilian destination] are better than most people expect. We curate only the best. This is one of them.
```

**CTA style:** "Explore stays" / "Plan your trip" / "See more properties"
**CTA button:** "Learn More" (lowest friction for cold)

**Landing page direction:** Property-specific or destination page — not homepage. The ad creates specific desire → the landing page must deliver on that specific desire.

**Logo in ad:** Bottom of image creative, Version A (full logo), Areia on dark. In carousel, prominent on final slide.

## FRAMEWORK 2 — VILLA DESIRE GENERATION

**Goal:** Make couples, families, and groups imagine themselves in a private villa in Brazil.

**Target audience:**
- Cold + Warm: Couples 28–50, families with teens, friend groups. Interests: villa rentals, Airbnb Luxe, VRBO luxury, boutique travel, honeymoon destinations.
- Comparison audience: people who have searched for Tulum villas, Bali villas, Greek island rentals, Ibiza villa holidays.

**Main emotion:** Privacy + freedom + escape

**Problem solved:** "I want a private experience, not a hotel corridor." / "I want space for my family / group." / "I want something more memorable than a standard hotel."

**Visual concept:** Private pool at golden hour, villa exterior among nature, terrace with view, hammock and ocean.

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "Your own corner of Brazil. Private villa, private pool, private Brazil."
B: "A villa in Trancoso for the trip you've been putting off."
C: "No lobby. No queue. No shared pool. Just your villa in Bahia."
D: "Brazil has villas that make every other destination feel obvious."
E: "Families, couples, and groups who want Brazil done differently."
```

**Primary text directions:**
```
Version A (desire-first):
There's a category of accommodation in Brazil that most international travelers never find: private villas tucked into the Atlantic rainforest, opening onto their own beach, with staff who cook, clean, and disappear when you want them to. Voyu knows where they are. We'll match you to the right one.

Version B (versus framing):
You've considered Ibiza. You've considered Tulum. Have you considered a private villa in Trancoso — where the village feels like it belongs to you, the beaches are uncrowded, and the experience costs a fraction of St. Barths? We'll find you the right property.

Version C (family/group):
When you travel with people you love, the accommodation isn't just where you sleep. It's where the trip actually happens. Voyu curates private villas across Brazil for families and groups who want space, privacy, and something worth the journey.
```

**CTA style:** "Find your villa" / "Explore Brazilian villas" / "Tell us your group"
**CTA button:** "Send Message" or "Learn More"

## FRAMEWORK 3 — BRAZIL DESTINATION DESIRE

**Goal:** Convert people who are undecided about Brazil — or who haven't considered it — into active trip planners.

**Target audience:**
- Cold: People who have shown interest in luxury travel, international destinations, specific comparison destinations (Ibiza, Tulum, Amalfi, Bali, Mykonos). Broad affluent demographics in US/EU.

**Main emotion:** Discovery + FOMO (tasteful)

**Problem solved:** "I've been going to the same destinations. Brazil is starting to sound interesting."

**Visual concept:** The most stunning aspect of each destination — Fernando de Noronha turquoise water, Trancoso Quadrado at sunset, Rio from a hotel terrace, Bahia coast from above.

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "You've thought about Ibiza. You haven't thought about Trancoso."
B: "The water in Fernando de Noronha will ruin every other beach you've ever been to."
C: "Brazil has a version of itself that rivals anywhere in the world. Most travelers never find it."
D: "What if your next trip made everywhere else feel like the second choice?"
E: "Maragogi, Alagoas. Turquoise water. Warm people. Zero crowds."
```

**Primary text directions:**
```
Version A (comparison):
Ibiza has its moment every summer. Tulum has had its moment. The next place that travelers who know where to go are choosing: Brazil. Not all of it — the right parts of it. The beaches in Alagoas with shallow turquoise water that rivals the Maldives. The villas in Trancoso with pools overlooking the Atlantic rainforest. The boutique hotels in Rio with terraces that look at Christ the Redeemer from the other side. Voyu knows where. We'll show you.

Version B (revelation):
If your idea of Brazil is carnival, soccer, and Rio — you're missing most of it. There's an entire coastline of boutique pousadas, private villas, and design hotels that international travelers have barely discovered. That's where we come in. Voyu curates the best stays in Brazil for travelers who know what they want.
```

**CTA:** "Discover Brazil" / "See our curated stays" / "Explore where to stay"

## FRAMEWORK 4 — TRUST & SERVICE DIFFERENTIATION

**Goal:** Convert someone who is already interested in Brazil but hasn't decided to use Voyu specifically.

**Target audience:**
- Warm: Website visitors, Instagram followers, email subscribers, people who've engaged with previous ads.

**Main emotion:** Confidence + relief

**Problem solved:** "I don't know who to trust." / "I've had bad experiences with travel agencies." / "I can research this myself — why would I use Voyu?"

**Visual concept:** Behind-the-scenes: team assessing property, real property photos (not stock), quote cards.

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "We've personally assessed every property we recommend. Here's why that matters."
B: "Not every hotel in Brazil is worth your time. We know which ones are."
C: "Voyu doesn't sell travel. We curate it."
D: "Your Brazil trip, handled by people who've been there, assessed it, and know the difference."
E: "The difference between booking and curating."
```

**Primary text directions:**
```
Version A (authority):
We've walked through the lobbies, slept in the beds, evaluated the service, and sat by the pools of [X] properties across Brazil. About [X]% didn't make our collection. The rest — the ones that actually deliver — are the properties Voyu recommends. If you're planning a trip to Brazil, you should know who you're booking with. That's us.

Version B (differentiation):
Booking.com shows you options. Voyu shows you the answer. We're not an agency. We're Brazil's curated accommodation specialist. We match you with the right hotel or villa — based on what you actually want — and handle the reservation, the details, and anything that comes up. Brazil, done right.
```

**CTA:** "See how Voyu works" / "Plan your stay →" / "Talk to our team"
**CTA button:** "Send Message" — this audience is warm enough for direct contact

## FRAMEWORK 5 — LEAD GENERATION (Direct Response)

**Goal:** Capture qualified leads — people who are actively planning a Brazil trip and want help with accommodation.

**Target audience:**
- Hot: People who've visited the Voyu website, engaged with 3+ posts, watched 50%+ of a video ad, or are in email nurture sequence.

**Main emotion:** Readiness + ease

**Problem solved:** "I'm actually planning this trip and I need to figure out where to stay."

**Visual concept:** Clean, brand-forward. Logo prominent. The ad looks like an invitation, not a disruption.

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "Planning a trip to Brazil? Let's start with where you'll stay."
B: "Ready to plan your Brazil stay? We're here."
C: "Tell us your destination. We'll send you our top picks."
D: "Your Brazil trip, planned by people who know every property."
E: "One conversation. The right stay in Brazil."
```

**Primary text:**
```
Short, direct, service-led:

Planning a trip to Brazil and not sure where to stay? That's exactly what Voyu is for.

Tell us your destination, your travel dates, and what you're looking for. We'll send you our curated recommendations within 24 hours.

No fees to inquire. Just expert curation, matched to you.

→ voyutravel.com or send us a DM.
```

**CTA:** "Get my recommendations" / "Plan my stay" / "Send a message"
**CTA button:** "Send Message" or link to inquiry form

## FRAMEWORK 6 — REMARKETING (Non-converters)

**Goal:** Re-engage people who visited the site or inquired but didn't book.

**Target audience:**
- Website visitors (last 30/60/90 days), incomplete form submissions, DM conversation starters who went silent.

**Main emotion:** Gentle reminder + relevant new content

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "Still thinking about Brazil? Here's what we'd recommend for [month of year]."
B: "The villa you looked at in Trancoso — it's still available."
C: "You've been thinking about Brazil. We've been thinking about the right stay for you."
D: "A new property just joined our collection. Thought you'd want to know."
```

**Ad approach:**
- Show a different property than what they saw before (or a new one)
- Reference the season/timing: "Perfect for travel in [Q3/Q4/etc.]"
- Remove friction: "No commitment to inquire. Just a conversation."

---

## FRAMEWORK 7 — WARM AUDIENCE CONVERSION (Email + Social Retargeting)

**Goal:** Convert email subscribers and engaged social followers into active inquirers.

**Target audience:**
- Custom audience: email list upload, Instagram engagers (last 90 days), video viewers (50%+)

**Ad approach:**
- More intimate tone — they already know Voyu
- Reference the relationship: "If you've been following Voyu, you know how we choose our properties."
- Introduce urgency without artifice: "Availability in [destination] is filling for [season]."
- Specific property or destination hook

**Headline directions:**
```
A: "For those of you planning Brazil this year: this is our latest recommendation."
B: "You've been following Voyu. Here's what we'd put you in for [destination]."
C: "Trancoso in [month] — these are the three properties we'd choose today."
```

## AD SPECS REFERENCE (Meta)

| Placement | Ratio | Resolution | Notes |
|-----------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Feed (image) | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | Primary format |
| Feed (square) | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | Fallback |
| Stories | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | Safe zone: keep content 14% from top/bottom |
| Reels Ad | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | First 3 sec critical |
| Carousel | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | 2–10 cards |
| Right column | 1.91:1 | 1200×628 | Desktop only, lower priority |

**Primary text:** 125 characters before truncation (first 125 chars must hook)
**Headline (below image):** 27 characters before truncation
**Description:** 27 characters (often not shown)
