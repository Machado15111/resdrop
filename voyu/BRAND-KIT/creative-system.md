# VOYU — Creative System
## Complete Format Guide for Posts, Carousels, Stories, Reels & Ads

---

## CREATIVE FORMAT INDEX

| Format | Primary Goal | Frequency |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Feed Post (single image) | Desire, inspiration, brand presence | 4–5x/week |
| Carousel | Education, curation, comparison | 2–3x/week |

## FORMAT 1 — FEED POST (Single Image / 4:5 ratio)

**Purpose:** Create desire. Anchor brand in the mind of someone who doesn't know Voyu yet.

**Best use cases:**
- Property showcase (hotel exterior, suite, pool, view)
- Destination mood (beach at golden hour, city vista, jungle light)
- Detail shots that tell a story (breakfast tray, hammock, cocktail, architecture)
- Quote/inspiration overlays

**Layout structure:**
```
┌────────────────────────────┐
│                            │
│      FULL BLEED IMAGE      │
│    (occupies 75–85% of     │
│         the frame)         │
│                            │
│   ┌────────────────────┐   │  ← Optional: subtle dark gradient at bottom (25% opacity)
│   │ HEADLINE (optional)│   │
│   └────────────────────┘   │
│   [VOYU LOGO — discreet]   │  ← Bottom-right or bottom-center. Small. Version C (icon) or A
└────────────────────────────┘
```

**Image style:** Full-bleed, editorial, warm. Architectural or landscape. Thin logo watermark.

**Text density:** Minimal on the image. 0–8 words max. The caption carries the message.

**Logo usage:** Version C (icon only) as subtle watermark, bottom-right corner. OR Version A (full logo) when image is dark enough for contrast. Size: ~12–15% of frame width.

**CTA style:** In caption, not on the image.

**Emotional role:** "I want to be there." Pure desire activation.

**Best audience:** Cold (discovery), warm (nurture). All audiences.

## FORMAT 2 — CAROUSEL (Multiple slides, 4:5 or 1:1)

**Purpose:** Educate, curate, compare. Reward followers who swipe.

**Best use cases:**
- "5 reasons to stay at [hotel]"
- "The best places to stay in [destination]"
- "[Destination] — what to know before you go, starting with where you'll sleep"
- "Hotel vs Villa — which is right for your Brazil trip"
- Neighborhood guide through accommodation
- "Before and after: how we planned this Trancoso stay"

**Slide structure:**
```
SLIDE 1 (COVER):
┌────────────────────────────┐
│    FULL BLEED HERO IMAGE   │
│                            │
│  ┌──────────────────────┐  │
│  │   HEADLINE — large   │  │  ← Cormorant Garamond, centered or left-aligned
│  │  (the promise/hook)  │  │
│  └──────────────────────┘  │
│  [VOYU LOGO — prominent]   │  ← Full logo, center-bottom or full width bottom bar
└────────────────────────────┘

SLIDES 2–N (CONTENT):
┌────────────────────────────┐
│   IMAGE (60% of frame)     │
├────────────────────────────┤
│  NUMBER / LABEL            │  ← Small, DM Sans, all-caps, tracking +150
│  Headline (1 sentence)     │  ← Cormorant Garamond, regular
│  Body (2–4 lines max)      │  ← DM Sans Light, small
└────────────────────────────┘

FINAL SLIDE (CTA):
┌────────────────────────────┐
│  Background: Noite         │
│                            │
│  [VOYU LOGO — centered]    │
│                            │
│  "Ready to plan your       │
│   stay in [destination]?"  │
│                            │
│  voyutravel.com            │
└────────────────────────────┘
```

**Text density:** Cover slide = minimal (headline only). Content slides = moderate (1 headline + 2–4 lines). Final slide = CTA only.

**Logo usage:** Full logo prominent on cover slide and final slide. Icon watermark on content slides (bottom-right, small).

**Visual consistency across slides:** Same color temperature edit on all images. Same font treatment. Consistent bottom-right logo placement on content slides.

**Emotional role:** "This brand knows more than I do. I should follow them — and contact them."

**Best audience:** Warm (followers, website visitors, email subscribers).

## FORMAT 3 — STORY SEQUENCE

**Purpose:** Daily brand presence, behind-the-scenes, quick CTAs, engagement.

**Story types and structures:**

### Type A — Property Showcase Story:
```
Story 1: Full-screen property image + destination label (top-left: DM Sans all-caps)
Story 2: Detail shot + 1 evocative sentence overlay
Story 3: CTA slide — "Planning a trip to [destination]?" + "DM us" or link sticker
```

### Type B — Behind-the-Scenes Story:
```
Story 1: Text-only opener on dark background: "We visited [X] properties in Bahia this month."
Story 2–4: Real photos from property assessment / FAM trip
Story 5: "Here's the one that made our list." + property teaser
Story 6: CTA — "See our Bahia picks → link in bio"
```

### Type C — Engagement Story:
```
Story 1: Question sticker — "Which would you choose for Trancoso?" [Villa A or B]
Story 2: Follow-up 24h later — "Here's what we'd choose — and why."
Story 3: CTA — "Thinking about Trancoso? DM us."
```

### Type D — Trust Story:
```
Story 1: Quote from a recent client (white text on dark background)
Story 2: The property they stayed at
Story 3: "This trip was planned in [X] days." + CTA
```

**Story design rules:**
- Safe zone: keep all text/logo 8% from edges (iOS/Android UI overlap)
- Logo: top-left corner (Version C — icon only) or text-based "VOYU" label
- Text: never more than 6 lines on a story
- Font: Cormorant Garamond for evocative lines, DM Sans for labels and CTAs
- Bottom: always room for link sticker (don't put important content there)

## FORMAT 4 — REEL COVER

**Purpose:** First impression on the grid. Must be beautiful as a static image even before the reel plays.

**Design rules:**
- Treat reel cover as a feed post — editorial, beautiful, intentional
- Best frame from the reel (or a dedicated shoot)
- Minimal text — destination name only, or no text
- Logo: icon only (Version C), bottom-right or top-left, very small (8–10% of frame)
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertically, but design so the center 4:5 crop works for the grid

**Cover categories:**
- **Destination cover:** Wide shot of location (no text, or destination name only)
- **Property cover:** Hero shot of hotel/villa exterior or key feature
- **Mood cover:** Detail, texture, atmosphere — no text needed
- **Titled cover:** Cormorant Garamond headline + destination label (for "guide" style reels)

## FORMAT 5 — STATIC AD (for Meta Ads)

**Purpose:** Lead generation, inquiry generation, remarketing.

**Ad type A — "Property Desire" (top-of-funnel cold):**
```
┌────────────────────────────┐
│  BEAUTIFUL PROPERTY IMAGE  │
│                            │
│  ┌──────────────────────┐  │
│  │ SHORT HEADLINE       │  │  ← Cormorant Garamond italic, 2–5 words
│  │ (evocative, minimal) │  │
│  └──────────────────────┘  │
│  [VOYU LOGO — bottom]      │
└────────────────────────────┘
Primary text (above image in feed): Desire-led caption, 40–80 words
CTA button: "Learn More" or "Send Message"
```

**Ad type B — "Curation Trust" (mid-funnel warm):**
```
Background: Noite (#0A0A0A)
Centered layout:

VOYU LOGO (centered, medium size)

"Where to stay in [destination]."
[property name + destination]

"Curated for travelers who know what they want."

[voyutravel.com]
```

**Ad type C — "Comparison" (warm audience retargeting):**
```
Split-image or carousel ad:
Left: Generic alternative destination (Ibiza beach, Tulum cenote)
Right: Brazilian equivalent (Trancoso village, Fernando de Noronha water)

Headline: "You've thought about Ibiza. Have you thought about Trancoso?"
Body: Short comparison — warmth, privacy, design, a fraction of the cost
CTA: "Explore stays →"
```

**Ad type D — "Social Proof" (remarketing):**
```
Quote from traveler on dark background
Small property image below/beside
"[Name], [origin], traveled with Voyu [month year]"
CTA: "Plan your stay →"
```

**Logo in all ads:** Always present. Bottom of creative or top-center. Never competing with the main image. Always legible.

## FORMAT 6 — VIDEO AD CONCEPT

**Purpose:** Cold audience demand generation (especially for Meta video ads).

### Video Ad Structure (15–30 seconds):

```
0:00–0:03 → HOOK FRAME
  Scene: The most visually arresting moment (pool at sunrise, turquoise water, private terrace)
  Text overlay (optional): One evocative line in Cormorant Garamond italic
  
0:03–0:15 → DESIRE BUILD
  Series of 3–5 beautiful shots: property, destination, detail
  No text — let the visual speak
  Music: Instrumental Brazilian jazz or ambient (low volume)
  
0:15–0:25 → BRAND + POSITIONING
  Text appears: "Where to stay in Brazil — curated."
  VOYU TRAVEL logo fades in (centered, Areia on dark)
  
0:25–0:30 → CTA
  Static end frame: Dark background, logo, voyutravel.com
  "Plan your stay →"
```

**For 60-second video ads (awareness):**
Add a story arc: introduce destination → show 2–3 properties → explain what Voyu does in 1 sentence → CTA.

## FORMAT 7 — TESTIMONIAL CREATIVE

**Purpose:** Build social proof and trust. Convert undecided prospects.

**Layout options:**

**Option A — Quote card (dark):**
```
Background: Noite with subtle texture or image underlay at 15% opacity
Quote: Cormorant Garamond italic, large, centered
  "We arrived and thought: this is exactly right."
Attribution: DM Sans Light — "[First name], [City, Country] · [Destination] · [Month Year]"
Logo: bottom-center, small
```

**Option B — Split layout:**
```
Left half: Property image
Right half: Dark background with quote text
Logo: bottom-right of dark half
```

**Option C — Story-style:**
```
Full-screen image of the property they stayed at
Overlay at bottom: frosted glass panel with quote
Attribution below quote
```

**What testimonials should say (guide clients):**
- Not: "Great service, will recommend!" → too generic
- Yes: "We had no idea where to stay in Rio. Voyu sent us to [hotel] and it was exactly what we needed — perfectly located, beautifully designed, service that anticipated everything."

## FORMAT 8 — BRAND AUTHORITY CREATIVE

**Purpose:** Establish credibility and expertise. "Why trust Voyu."

**Layout:** Dark background. Logo prominent. Minimal, confident text.

**Examples:**

```
Creative A:
Background: Noite
[VOYU LOGO — centered, medium]
"[X] properties across Brazil.
Personally assessed.
Curated for travelers like you."
[voyutravel.com]

Creative B:
Background: Beautiful property image (dark-toned)
Text overlay:
"We've stayed in [X] hotels in Rio.
Here are the [3/5] we keep recommending."
[Logo]

Creative C:
Background: Noite
Large number: "47"
Subtitle: "properties personally assessed in 2024"
Body: "Not every hotel makes our list. Here's what does."
[Logo + voyutravel.com]
```
