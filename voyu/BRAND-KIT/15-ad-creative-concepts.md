## PRODUCTION NOTES FOR ALL ADS

**Color correction standard:** Before submitting any ad creative for production, check:
- ✅ Warm highlight tone (no cold blues in the image highlights)
- ✅ Contrast legibility of logo (Areia on dark or Noite on light)
- ✅ Text-free safety zone around logo
- ✅ Brand colors used: Noite, Areia, Creme only (no off-brand colors added)
- ✅ Single message per creative — no competing headlines or CTAs

**Testing protocol:**
For each campaign, test minimum 3 creative variants:
- Variant A: Evocative, image-first (desire)
- Variant B: Authority claim, copy-heavy (trust)
- Variant C: Direct response (conversion)

Let the data decide — but never test something that's off-brand.
