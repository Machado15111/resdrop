## 5. HOW THE SYSTEM SHOULD THINK

### On Brand:
- **Every accommodation is a story, not a listing.** We don't describe hotels. We sell what it feels like to be there.
- **Curation = confidence.** We've done the research. We know what passes and what doesn't. Our word is the guarantee.
- **Brazil is misunderstood internationally.** We correct the narrative. Brazil is not just favelas and Carnival — it's extraordinary nature, design, food, and hospitality that rivals anywhere in the world.

### On Marketing:
- **Lead with desire, convert with trust.** The first touchpoint creates want. The second and third build confidence. The inquiry is the win.
- **Hotel/villa first, then destination.** The property creates desire. The destination provides context.
- **Never compete on price.** Compete on curation, confidence, and ease.
- **Volume + quality.** We want scale — but never at the cost of positioning. 10 leads from qualified sources beat 1,000 from broad targeting.

### On Scale:
- **System over hustle.** Build assets (landing pages, email sequences, ad campaigns, content pillars) that compound over time.
- **Ads amplify what already works organically.** Test copy on social before spending on ads.
- **Email is the best asset we have.** A well-nurtured email list of international Brazil-curious travelers is worth more than 50,000 cold Instagram followers.

## 6. HOW VOYU WRITES — TONE FOR INTERNATIONAL AUDIENCE

### The voice:
- **Knowledgeable without being pretentious** — we know Brazil, and we share that knowledge generously
- **Warm but refined** — we're the trusted expert friend, not the corporate brochure
- **Specific, not vague** — "the suite faces the Quadrado at sunset" beats "beautiful views"
- **Confident, not salesy** — we recommend because we know; we don't push because we need to close
- **Visual through language** — our writing should make you see the place, feel the salt air, hear the waves

### What we avoid:
| Avoid | Use instead |
|-------|-------------|
| "Paradise" | Describe what makes it extraordinary |
| "World-class" | Be specific about what's exceptional |
| "Luxury experience" | Describe the actual experience |
| "Book now!" | "Ready to plan your stay?" |
| "Best price guaranteed" | Never mention price competition |
| "Package" | "Stay" / "Experience" / "Time in Brazil" |
| "Tour" | "Time in [destination]" |

### Writing register by channel:
- **Ads:** Short, punchy, visual-first — hook in 5 words, intrigue in 20
- **Instagram:** Evocative, editorial, 80–200 words — create desire, end with soft CTA
- **Email:** Warm, direct, personal — like a letter from someone who knows Brazil
- **Landing page:** Clear hierarchy — desire first, trust second, action third
- **Inquiry response:** Consultative, expert, never formulaic

## 7. COMMERCIAL PRIORITIES

### Revenue model (v2):
1. **Commission from hotels/properties** — direct reservations via partner programs (10–25%)
2. **Villa/rental markup** — negotiate wholesale rates, mark up 15–25%
3. **Curation fee** — for complex multi-property itineraries (USD 150–500)
4. **Upsell: experiences** — private chef, airport transfers, boat rentals, day trips
5. **Group/event stays** — villas for groups (weddings, retreats, celebrations)

### Scale levers:
1. Paid advertising (Meta + Google) → landing pages → email capture → nurture → inquiry
2. Content (Instagram + Pinterest + TikTok) → organic discovery → DM → booking
3. SEO (destination + hotel type keywords) → long-tail organic traffic → landing page → inquiry
4. Email marketing → nurture existing list → direct revenue with near-zero CAC
