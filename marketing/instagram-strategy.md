## Instagram Ad Strategy

### Format Mix
| Format | Share | Purpose |
|--------|-------|---------|
| Reels | 50% | Reach, viral potential, algorithm boost |
| Stories | 25% | Conversions, urgency, swipe-up installs |
| Carousels | 20% | Education, feature showcases |
| Static/Collection | 5% | Retargeting, social proof |

### Content Pillars
1. **Savings Proof (40%)** - Real savings screenshots, weekly roundups, before/after comparisons
2. **Travel Hacks (25%)** - When to book, how pricing works, money-saving tips
3. **Destination Inspiration (20%)** - Beautiful hotels + "save X% with ResDrop"
4. **Behind the Scenes (10%)** - How ResDrop works, founder story, features
5. **Trending/Viral (5%)** - Trending audio with travel twist, memes

### Reels Concepts
1. "POV: You get a price drop alert at the beach" - Relaxed scene, phone notification, savings reveal
2. "Hotel price secrets they don't want you to know" - Educational, quick cuts, text overlays
3. "I saved R$1,150 in 3 seconds" - Screen recording showing alert + rebooking flow

## Growth Hacking Strategies

### 1. WhatsApp Referral Program (Highest Priority)
- Two-sided: Referrer gets 1 month premium free, referee gets 1 month free
- One-tap share via WhatsApp (critical for Brazil)
- Expected: 22-25% referral rates

### 2. Savings Calculator Viral Tool
- Free web tool: "How much could you have saved?"
- User enters hotel, dates, price -> shows estimated savings
- Shareable result (like Spotify Wrapped for travel)

### 3. Micro-Influencer Strategy
- Partner with 5-10 Brazilian travel micro-influencers (5K-50K followers)
- Free premium + affiliate commission per install
- Focus on budget/smart travel creators

### 4. "Price Drop Challenge" UGC Campaign
- Users share biggest savings on social media
- Monthly winner gets a free hotel night
- Generates user-generated content at scale

### 5. SEO/Content Play
- Blog targeting "hotel [destination] preco" keywords
- Long-tail Portuguese keywords with low competition
- Each article funnels to app install
